## About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at [HKUST-GZ](https://www.hkust-gz.edu.cn/), supervised by [Prof. Harry Shum](https://ias.hkust.edu.hk/people/ias-members/alumni/prof-harry-heung-yeung-shum) and [Prof. Lionel Ni](https://facultyprofiles.hkust-gz.edu.cn/faculty-personal-page/NI-LionelMing-Shuan/ni), working on **autoregressive visual generation** and **interactive world models**.

My journey started with NLP at **Tencent AI Lab**, then shifted to visual generation at **Xiaobing**, where I built one-shot talking head systems (ICCV 2023). At **StepFun**, I worked on large-scale visual generation — contributing to the 30B-parameter [Step-Video-T2V](https://github.com/stepfun-ai/Step-Video-T2V) <span style="display: inline-block; background: linear-gradient(135deg, #fef3c7, #fde68a); padding: 1px 7px; border-radius: 10px; font-size: 12px; font-weight: 600; color: #92400e; vertical-align: middle;"><i class="fas fa-star" style="font-size: 11px;"></i> 3k+</span>, pioneering **frame-level autoregressive** video generation ([MAGI](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.12389), CVPR 2025), and ultimately building [NextStep-1](https://github.com/stepfun-ai/NextStep-1) — the first **autoregressive** image model with continuous tokens that matches diffusion quality at scale (ICLR 2026 Oral) <span style="display: inline-block; background: linear-gradient(135deg, #fef3c7, #fde68a); padding: 1px 7px; border-radius: 10px; font-size: 12px; font-weight: 600; color: #92400e; vertical-align: middle;"><i class="fas fa-star" style="font-size: 11px;"></i> 600+</span>.

I'm always excited to discuss new ideas, especially the *crazy* ones. Don't hesitate to get in touch!
